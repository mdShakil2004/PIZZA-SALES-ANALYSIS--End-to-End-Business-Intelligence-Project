### Business Problem

Pizza restaurant chains operate in a highly competitive market with thin margins. Management needs clear visibility into **sales performance**, **product popularity**, **seasonality**, and **operational efficiency** to drive revenue growth, optimize inventory, and improve customer satisfaction.

**Key Objectives:**
- Calculate core KPIs (Revenue, Order Volume, Average Order Value)
- Identify sales trends by time, category, and size
- Highlight top and bottom performers
- Provide interactive dashboards for stakeholders

### Dataset

- **Source**: Pizza Sales Dataset (2015) – Kaggle
- **Structure**: Orders, order details, pizzas, and pizza types tables
- **Time Period**: Full year 2015
- **Key Fields**: Order ID, Date, Time, Quantity, Unit Price, Pizza Category, Size, Ingredients

### Key Metrics Analyzed

| Metric                    | Definition                                      | Business Value                     |
|---------------------------|-------------------------------------------------|------------------------------------|
| **Total Revenue**         | Sum of (Quantity × Unit Price)                  | Overall financial performance      |
| **Total Orders**          | Count of unique orders                          | Demand volume                      |
| **Total Pizzas Sold**     | Sum of quantities sold                          | Production & inventory needs       |
| **Average Order Value**   | Total Revenue ÷ Total Orders                    | Customer spending behavior         |
| **Average Pizzas per Order** | Total Pizzas Sold ÷ Total Orders             | Order size & bundling effectiveness|
| **Sales by Category & Size** | Revenue and volume breakdown                 | Product mix optimization           |

### Methodology & Project Phases

#### 1. Data Acquisition & SQL Analysis
- Loaded and normalized data in **Microsoft SQL Server**
- Developed optimized queries for:
  - Revenue aggregation
  - Time-based trend analysis (daily, weekly, monthly, hourly)
  - Category & size performance
  - Top/bottom sellers
- Implemented window functions and CTEs for advanced analytics

#### 2. Excel Dashboard
- Built dynamic **Pivot Tables** and **Pivot Charts**
- Created an interactive Excel dashboard with slicers, timelines, and conditional formatting
- Focused on self-service analysis for non-technical stakeholders

#### 3. Power BI Dashboard
- Established robust **data model** with relationships and DAX measures
- Implemented interactive visuals, drill-throughs, and bookmarks
- Added KPI cards, trend lines, and decomposition trees for root-cause analysis

#### 4. Tableau Visualization
- Designed a professional multi-page dashboard with **parameter actions** and **calculated fields**
- Created advanced views including:
  - Daily/Weekly/Monthly order trends
  - Hourly peak analysis
  - Best & Worst Sellers (with percentage contribution)
  - Category performance heatmaps

### Key Insights & Visualizations

- **Temporal Trends**: Identified clear **peak ordering hours** (evening rush) and **weekly patterns** (weekends outperform weekdays).
- **Product Performance**: Certain categories and sizes dominate revenue; highlighted high-margin opportunities and underperformers for potential menu optimization.
- **Order Behavior**: Average order value and pizzas-per-order metrics revealed bundling effectiveness.
- **Seasonality**: Monthly trends provided guidance for promotional planning and staffing.

**Visualizations Included:**
- Bar & Line charts for time-series trends
- Pie/Donut charts for category distribution
- Treemaps and heatmaps for performance ranking
- Interactive filters and tooltips for exploration
